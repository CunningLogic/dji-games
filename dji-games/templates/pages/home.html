Game Rules

How to Win

The DJI Games challenges you to fly a Phantom 3 through a free fly obstacle course, earning points for each maneuver you complete within a 2-minute time limit. You are not required to complete every obstacle. You have the freedom to strategize based on your own skill sets. Only those with the highest scores can continue competing to win DJI Prizes and glory.

Day 1: Qualifying Rounds

During the qualifying rounds, each competitor will be flying solo to determine his/her own individual skill.

6 contestants per round

Day 2: Semi Finals

In the semi-final rounds, competitors will be randomly paired with one-another to test his/her ability to work as a team.

2 contestants per team, 3 teams per round

Day 2: Finals

During the final rounds, competitors will be randomly assigned with a different team member. Teams will be given additional practice time prior to the rounds so that that they can devise a strategy based on each member’s skill sets.

2 contestants per team, 3 teams per round

The Course

This DJI-designed course has several options and ways for you to earn points. Each participant will have their own course, and these maneuvers can be completed in any order, depending on your preference:

DJI Pyramid | 10-20 Points

Use the landing gear on the DJI Phantom to pick up the Pyramid, then place it down on the nearby bulls-eye. Where the Pyramid is placed determines your earned points. With 10, 15, or 20 points up for grabs, this is your biggest single opportunity to earn the most points.

The Pyramid can only be used once per participant per run.

Landing Pads | 2-5 Points

Land your Phantom on each landing pad and stop on the ground for 1 second. Where you land your Phantom determines the number of points earned – either 5 points for the center, or 2 points for the edge.

Each landing pad can only be used once per participant per run.

Figure-8 | 4 Points

Complete a figure-8 motion around the two pylons in the center of the course. Each completed figure-8 earns you 4 points.

Figure 8s can be completed an unlimited number of times during each run.

Loops | 2 Points

Fly through the raised loop on top of each pylon to earn 2 points.

Each loop can only be used twice per participant per run.

Time Limit

Any of the above maneuvers must be completed before the time for your run expires to earn the associated points. Time limits for each run will vary depending on which round of the DJI Games you advance to.

Penalties

To encourage safe flying and adherence to the rules, points will be subtracted from participants as follows:

Crashing | -15 Points, End Run

Flying your Phantom into the ground or other objects, causing it to be unable to continue flying on its own, results in an immediate end to your run. 15 points will be deducted from your current score, and you will not be able to earn any further points.

Out of Bounds | -10 Points, End Run

Flying your Phantom outside of the designated area for your course or interfering/flying into a course being used by other participants will result in an immediate end to your run. 10 points will be deducted from your current score, and you will not be able to earn any further points.

Failure to Land Safely | -10 Points

After time expires for each run, all participants must land their Phantom safely on the ground within their course area and power off the motors within 15 seconds. Failure to land safely results in a 10-point deduction from your score.

Disqualification

Any behavior or flight conducted in an unsportsmanlike or inappropriate way will result in immediate, complete disqualification from the DJI Games and any future events. Decisions made by DJI officials on-site are final.
